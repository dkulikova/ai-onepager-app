# Mistral AI research notes

Mission & positioning:
Mistral AI is a Paris-headquartered frontier AI company focused on open models, enterprise AI, custom assistants and deployment flexibility. It is a useful European counterpoint to US-based AI labs.

Products and commercial context:
- Vibe / Le Chat is Mistral's AI assistant and agent product for work and code.
- La Plateforme / Studio supports custom agents, AI apps, observability and deployment control.
- Forge supports custom model development and fine-tuning.
- Mistral emphasises deployment portability from edge to cloud.

Leadership notes:
- Arthur Mensch is co-founder and CEO.
- Guillaume Lample is co-founder and Chief Science Officer.
- Timothée Lacroix is co-founder and CTO.

Caveats:
- Product naming has changed over time, so older references to Le Chat may map to Vibe.
- Revenue and customer figures are not fully disclosed and should be treated carefully.
